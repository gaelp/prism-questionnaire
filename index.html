About our project

We are researching the relationship between religion, spiritual practice and peacebuilding from the 18th century onwards and any 'ripple effect' of this in wider society.

We are interested in people and movements (including very small and local) associated with peacebuilding, especially within Christianity and Buddhism (broadly defined). We are also researching whether any of these figures/movements might be classified as mystics/mystical.

All the questions relate to any people/movements you know associated with peace/peacebuilding.
